looking for 3d artists

A 3D artist, also known as a CGI artist, creates 3D digital art using computer software to create models, animations, and visual effects for various creative projects: Tasks 3D artists design, model, and edit 3D art, including characters, environments, special effects, and lighting. They may also create storyboards and collaborate with other creative professionals. Tools 3D artists use 3D modeling software like 3ds Max, V-Ray, and Adobe Creative Suite. Skills 3D artists need a blend of technical proficiency and artistic sensibility. They should be skilled with CAD programs, have advanced-level rendering and 3D modeling abilities, and be able to collaborate with others. Education While formal education isn't strictly required, a bachelor's degree in art, computer graphics, or a multimedia field is recommended. Work environment 3D artists work with project managers, art directors, and other artists. They may also work with the sales team, production team, and tech art and design team.
